Our Story

Who We Are

The Local Grill Kenya continues the legacy set by our South African counterparts in 2002. We have been operating in Kenya since 2018 and have been fortunate to share our beef journey with many farmers , butchers, and customers around Kenya. We ensure quality by following strict “field to fork” principles, through sourcing ethical and sustainable beef, collaborated rearing, conscientious butchering, and cutting edge beef aging strategies.

This collaboration with producers has meant that over the years, we have been able to showcase different breeds from different regions and improve the offering year on year.
